    NEWS // APP

    Me and a team of other Developers came together to create an App called GoodMorning! It is a one stop shop for all your favorite trusted news sources, so you can view all your news in one convenient location! I was responsible for fetching the dynamic data to create the news article elements, and at first it was difficult because certain API's were clashing with Github's HTTPS Protocol and giving me an error of 426, which was pretty scary!
